JAPAN

Normally modern and traditional won’t come together, but not in Japan. Japan has a modernized city with well-maintained culture. You can have purest Japanese cuisine, most-polite service and japan traditional costume at the same time, which these were maintained from thousands year ago. Other than that, there are two famous scenarios in Japan winter season. You could visit mountain Fuji for the spectacular view or visit the Zoo for seeing the animal plays with the snow.

THAILAND

If you’re planning to travel country nearby, Thailand might be the best choice. In Thailand, there’s a different type of market you can go, such as wet market, night market and the special one, floating market. The floating market located at the main river in Thailand, then the seller used boats as their selling stalls and sell food or other stuff to the people who cross by the river by boats. Furthermore, there’s tons of fun activity in Thailand, especially elephant feeding and rafting.
